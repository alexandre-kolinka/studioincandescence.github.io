Project 01 · Objects
Prototype

crOOZin — LED PCB

Custom LED electronics designed for biomorphic 3D-printed light sculptures. A circuit board that disappears into the geometry — engineered for beauty as much as function.

The crOOZin PCB is designed to integrate seamlessly into the 3D-printed resin shell of the luminaire — a circular board that sits at the base, housing the LED driver, dimming circuit and USB-C power management in a footprint of less than 40mm diameter.

The board drives a warm white LED at 2700K with full TRIAC dimming, and integrates a dichroic lens mount for the signature bicolour effect. Future versions will include tunable white (2700K–6500K) and RGB capability for the colour series.

Project 02 · Therapeutic
R&D

Phyllotaxis — Therapeutic Lamp

A sculptural light instrument for stress regulation and wellbeing. Custom PCB integrating high-density micro-LEDs in Fibonacci spiral distribution, with biometric sensor feedback and embedded AI for adaptive light sequences.

Micro-LEDs are arranged using a phyllotaxis algorithm — Fibonacci spiral distribution — across a deployable parabolic structure of laser-cut petals. Each petal acts as both structural rib and LED carrier.

The control board integrates heart rate and breath sensors, feeding real-time biometric data to an on-device AI that modulates light sequences — spirals, pulses, waves — to guide the user toward coherence breathing and deep relaxation.

Sessions of 10–20 minutes. Target markets: corporate wellbeing, spa & hospitality, private clinics, meditation studios.

Project 03 · Sensory
Concept

Sensory — Headset

Micro-LED barrettes integrated into a wearable sensory headset — designed to create hypnotic light experiences in the peripheral and direct visual field. Light as a neurological tool.

A series of flexible micro-LED strips, custom-designed to conform to the interior geometry of a wearable headset. Each strip carries individually addressable LEDs at high density — capable of producing patterns, gradients and animated sequences that engage the visual cortex directly.

The system is designed to work in conjunction with sound and breath — creating a multi-sensory environment for focus, relaxation or altered states. Control via embedded microcontroller with wireless sync.

Project 04 · Hybrid Optics
R&D

LED + Fibre Optic

Hybrid light bars combining LED sources with fibre optic distribution — enabling light to travel through flexible structures, around curves and into geometries impossible with conventional LED strips.

Custom LED driver boards designed to couple high-intensity point sources into bundles of plastic optical fibre (POF). The fibre bundles are then integrated into 3D-printed or CNC-milled structural elements — creating light paths that follow the form rather than dictating it.

Applications range from architectural detail lighting to sculptural installations where conventional LED placement is impossible. The hybrid approach allows colour mixing at the source while delivering pure, controllable light at the end of the fibre.

Project 05 · Marine · Autonomous
Concept

Sea Swarm — Autonomous Drones

A constellation of small autonomous floating light drones — self-positioning around a vessel using swarm intelligence and phyllotaxis distribution. The ocean as a light installation.

Each drone unit carries a custom PCB integrating a focused LED source, underwater-rated optics, wireless communication module, GPS/IMU positioning, and a small battery. The swarm algorithm coordinates unit positions in real time, maintaining Fibonacci-inspired spatial distribution relative to the host vessel.

The electronic challenge: waterproofing to IP68, low-power consumption for extended battery life, mesh network communication between units, and position-aware adaptive lighting behaviour.
